About me

I'm a research associate worked with A/Prof. Jia Wu at School of Computing, Macquarie University. I studied at MQ for my PhD advised by A/Prof. Jia Wu and Prof. Jian Yang. Research interests: Graph Neural Networks, Graph Anomaly Detection, Brain Network Analysis.
